# Challenge Lifecycle

Challenges move through states based on availability, prerequisites, and policy flags.

## Visibility Rules

- Visible and unlocked: challenge appears normally with full detail.
- Hidden by prerequisites: challenge is excluded from normal listing.
- Anonymized by policy: challenge remains listed with obfuscated data.

## Prerequisite Semantics

A prerequisite chain controls when downstream challenges can be solved.

1. Team solves required upstream challenge(s).
2. Backend recalculates eligibility.
3. Newly unlocked challenges become visible or de-anonymized.

## Why This Matters

- Prevents accidental progression skipping
- Preserves intended game pacing
- Keeps scoring integrity under concurrent solves
