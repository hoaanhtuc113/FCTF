# Analytics and Incentives

This section describes how administrators govern fairness outcomes and strategic event insight.

## Submission Moderation

**Business objective:** Correct score-impacting anomalies fairly and transparently.

**Operational steps**

1. Filter submissions by challenge, team, user, and incident window.
2. Review evidence from submission history and support context.
3. Apply correction only when policy criteria are satisfied.
4. Record moderation reason and operator responsibility.
5. Validate ranking/score impact after correction.

**Expected result:** Dispute resolution is evidence-based and consistent.

## Scoreboard Intelligence and Event Analytics

**Business objective:** Monitor competition quality and identify performance outliers.

**Operational steps**

1. Review full standings and movement trends.
2. Segment performance by bracket or participant cohort.
3. Inspect challenge-level metrics: solve rate, failure density, hint usage.
4. Detect anomalies that may indicate fairness or clarity issues.
5. Feed insights into challenge operations and support prioritization.

**Expected result:** Organizers gain actionable visibility into event health.

## Rewards Engine

**Business objective:** Deliver transparent and repeatable reward outcomes.

**Operational steps**

1. Define reward objective and policy boundaries.
2. Configure eligibility criteria and tie-break conditions.
3. Preview candidate recipients before publication.
4. Validate result set with jury and governance rules.
5. Publish final outcomes and archive criteria for audit.

**Expected result:** Rewards are trusted, explainable, and policy-aligned.
